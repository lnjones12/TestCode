Time to Upgrade!

Why are you seeing this message?

It looks like you're trying to access CompetitorWireless.com from Internet Explorer 6. Due to the use of interactive elements on this site, we've found older versions of Internet Explorer to be incompatible with our website.

Okay, what do I do then?

Fear not, there are lots of ways for you to continue to access our site
Option 1 - You can run software update from your computer, and upgrade your browser to either Internet Explorer 7 or 8
Option 2 - You can download Firefox, Safari for the PC or Opera web browsers.

Where can I find these other browsers

We're glad you asked! Here's a list of links to get you started

You can download Internet Explorer 7 directly from Microsoft. if you're ready for Internet Explorer 8 You can download that as well.

IE not your flavor? Try out Firefox for a totally different browswer experience.

We want to make sure your experience on Competitor Wireless was the best we could make it. Unfortunately that meant discontinuing support for IE 6. We hope you understand, and look forward to servicing you after downloading an alternative browser.
